Our
Mission

We are on a mission to democratise data analytics and help the small & medium size businesses achieve similar or better results which were till now was a monopoly of companies with dedicated data science teams. We help you understand your data and augment your day to day decision making for higher profitability. We use advanced data science techniques based on cutting edge research to provide products and update them as the time and environment changes, so you and your business will be a step ahead in the brave new world of data science.

Data Maximus is a boutique data science research and consultancy company headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. We are a team of experienced data scientist with combined experience of over 40 years in some of the most innovative company in different geographies. We believe that advances in automation, data intelligence, and machine learning will augment and enhance human decision-making capabilities to a new high. We aim to achieve it through honesty, integrity, and commitment as our main guiding principle.
